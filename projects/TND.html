Project

Lynk & Co Next Day Interior

A full interior rebuild of the Lynk & Co The Next Day concept car in polygonal modeling, using hybrid techniques to resolve perforated and patterned surfaces. Built from a supplied frame, floor, and exterior mesh, guided by design sketches and hero shots of the car.
