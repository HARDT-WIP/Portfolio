HARDT.WIP

The personal side of Samuel Engelhardt: always in progress, on purpose.

It started as a small creative hub, helping people close by with graphic design projects whenever time allowed. That habit grew into Mixtape, a sub-brand made to mix every interest in one place, and Mixtape has since matured into Hardt.wip. The starting point stays part of the story, but the space itself is being rebuilt from the ground up, exactly as the name promises.
