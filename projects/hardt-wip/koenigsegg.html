Personal Project

Koenigsegg CC850

The Koenigsegg CC850, a modern homage to the CC8S that put Koenigsegg on the map, has always been one of my favorite cars for its aesthetics and design. I modeled the entire car myself in my free time, pushing the surfacing until it matched the official press photos.

Role
Personal Project
Period
2026
Focus
Surface Refinement & Visualization
Software
Blender, Alias
How I Built It
  1. 1

    Full Build

    Modeled the entire CC850 myself in Blender, using official press photos as the starting point and the goal was to get as close to them as possible.

  2. 2

    Precision Sections

    Where the surfacing needed to be exact, built precise NURBS sections in Alias and brought them back into Blender.

  3. 3

    Surface Continuity

    Used shrink wrap to blend the Alias sections into the surrounding surfaces, tightening highlights and continuity until it read like the official press photos.
