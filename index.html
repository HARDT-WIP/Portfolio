About

Bridging design and technical production.

I’m a Surface Designer & Digital Modeler from Visby, Sweden, currently based in Fribourg, Switzerland. With a background in automotive design, I work across digital modeling, Class A surfacing, and CAS development—combining technical precision with clean, purposeful design.

Passionate about 3D modeling and CAD, I thrive at the intersection of design, engineering, and production feasibility. Being part of the process where creativity meets technical execution is what inspires me most, and I take great pride in transforming design intent into refined, production-ready geometry.

The pursuit of high-quality craftsmanship fuels my drive to keep learning, evolving, and applying new techniques. Whether meticulously adjusting control points in NURBS-based software like Alias or shaping seamless polygonal patches in Blender, I’m driven by innovation, problem-solving, and the endless possibilities of digital design.

I believe learning never stops, and I’m committed to continuous growth—both personal and professional. Long term, I aim to keep pushing my capabilities further, taking on broader responsibility within product development and technical design across performance-driven industries.
